Frequently Asked Questions

Is SKY TOWER Hotel centrally located?

Yes, our hotel is located in the heart of Batumi, within walking distance to the coastline and main tourist attractions.

What is the view from the rooms?

Most of our rooms offer panoramic sea views. You can watch the unique Black Sea sunset from the comfort of your room.

Are parking and Wi-Fi free of charge?

Yes, high-speed wireless internet (Wi-Fi) and secure parking are completely free for all our guests.

Are there pool and spa facilities in the hotel?

Yes, our hotel features a luxury indoor swimming pool and a modern spa center open year-round.
