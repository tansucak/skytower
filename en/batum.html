Batumi Guide

Discover the Historical and Touristic Riches of Batumi, the Pearl of the Black Sea.

Batumi: Meeting of History and Modernity

Batumi is the capital of the Autonomous Republic of Adjara in Georgia and one of the most popular tourism centers on the Black Sea coast.

Enjoy staying in the heart of Batumi with many tourist spots within walking distance of our hotel.

Must-See Places

Ali and Nino Statue

Representing lovers who cannot meet, this iconic steel statue offers a fascinating show by being illuminated every evening.

Batumi Botanical Garden

One of the largest and richest botanical gardens in the world, this area is home to thousands of plant species.

Piazza Square

With its Italian-inspired architecture, mosaics and live music, it is one of the most pleasant meeting points in Batumi.

Batumi Boulevard

Established in 1881 and stretching for 7 kilometers, Batumi Boulevard reflects the soul of the city with its coastline and modern parks.

  • Alphabet Tower
  • Chacha Tower
  • Europe Square
  • Batumi Lighthouse

What to Eat in Batumi?

Discover the unique flavors of Georgian cuisine. Embark on a gastronomic journey with Adjaruli Khachapuri, Khinkali, and world-famous Georgian wines.

Batumi Nightlife

With its glittering casinos, exclusive beach clubs and modern rooftop bars, Batumi is known as the Las Vegas of the Black Sea.
