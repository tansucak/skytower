About Us

Located in Batumi, SKY TOWER Hotel offers modern accommodation units near the Black Sea coast. The hotel is 50 meters from Ardagani Lake. It offers a spa center, private parking, and free WiFi access. All rooms have sea and lake views and are elegantly decorated. Each room has air conditioning, a flat-screen satellite TV, a desk, and a minibar for your comfort. Complimentary bathrobes, slippers, toiletries, and a hairdryer are provided in the bathroom. You can enjoy a pleasant time sipping your drinks at the on-site bar. The hotel's reception is open 24 hours a day. SKY TOWER Hotel is 1.3 km from the Dolphinarium and 1.9 km from the Batumi Archaeological Museum. Batumi International Airport, the nearest airport, is 4 km away.
